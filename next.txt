4) RECOMMENDED "SMART" MEMBER LIFECYCLE MODEL
==================================================

Use this simple model:
- Active: full team access + assignable.
- Inactive: no team access + not assignable + preserved history.
- Reactivated: access restored, optionally auto-reassign pending tasks.

Recommended owner actions:
- Deactivate member.
- Reactivate member.
- Reassign all tasks from X -> Y (bulk).
- Archive member history.

Do NOT:
- Delete user account on team deactivation.
- Lose task history when membership changes.
