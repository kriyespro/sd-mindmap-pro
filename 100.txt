NTARQUE 100-PERSON TEAM MANAGEMENT PLAN (SIMPLE, NO-CODE)

Goal
- Run a 100-person team with clear ownership, fast decisions, and low meeting overhead.
- Focus on revenue and reliability first, based on current project priorities.

1) TEAM STRUCTURE (5 STREAMS)
- Stream A: Billing and Monetization (billing/)
- Stream B: Core Product and Planner Experience (planner/, teams/)
- Stream C: Users, Access, and Membership Lifecycle (users/)
- Stream D: Platform, Deploy, and Reliability (core/, Docker, CI)
- Stream E: QA, Release, and Customer Feedback Loop

Each stream must have:
- 1 owner (decision-maker)
- 1 backup owner
- up to 20 members max

2) SINGLE PAGE COMMAND CENTER (UPDATE WEEKLY)
Track only these fields:
- Top 3 priorities per stream
- Owner and backup
- KPI target vs current
- Blockers older than 24h
- This week: shipped / slipped / reason

3) WEEKLY OPERATING CADENCE
- Monday (30 min): lock weekly priorities and owners
- Wednesday (15 min): cross-team blocker removal
- Friday (30 min): KPI outcome review and next-step alignment

Meeting rules:
- No status storytelling
- Only decisions, blockers, and outcomes
- Anything unresolved gets owner + deadline

4) DECISION AND ESCALATION RULES
- Blocked >24h: escalate to stream owner
- Blocked >48h: escalate to project leadership
- Blocked >72h: priority reset in leadership sync

Decision rights:
- Product scope: Product owner + leadership
- Technical approach: stream owner
- Release go/no-go: QA + Platform owner
- Incident response: Platform owner (immediate)

5) KPI STACK FOR THIS PROJECT
Revenue KPIs
- Team-to-Team Pro upgrade rate
- Invite acceptance rate
- Time-to-first-invite

Reliability KPIs
- Billing action error rate
- Failed deploys per month
- PDF/CSV export failure rate

Execution KPIs
- Percent tasks with active valid assignee
- Weekly completion ratio by stream
- Critical blocker resolution time

6) 30-DAY ROLLOUT
Week 1
- Finalize 5 streams
- Assign owners and backups
- Publish command center

Week 2
- Start cadence (Mon/Wed/Fri)
- Enforce escalation rules
- Identify top 10 blockers

Week 3
- Align work to P0 items:
  - billing flow reliability
  - seat-limit guardrails
  - deploy safety checks

Week 4
- Measure KPIs
- Remove process noise
- Keep only rituals that improve delivery

7) KEEP IT SIMPLE
- Use existing tools only (GitHub + current docs)
- No new heavy management software now
- No extra meetings unless tied to a blocker or decision

Outcome expected in 30 days
- Faster delivery with less confusion
- Better accountability across 100 people
- Revenue and reliability priorities shipped with fewer surprises
